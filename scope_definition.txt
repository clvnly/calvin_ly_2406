14. What information system is being proposed to be designed?
A Transaction Processing System that speeds up the customer order process. Customers can also track their order via GPS.

15. Describe why the company needs it and why the company would be better off after it is in place.
The company previously received customer orders through email. This proved to be inefficient and cumbersome for hundreds of orders every day.
The information system provides the company with a reliable and easy to use system that would greatly improve the sales transaction process,
as opposed to working off of customer emails and traditional pen and paper.

16. Provide an estimate of how long it will take to design, implement, and install it.
1 month to plan, 2 months of analysis, 4 months to design, 3 months to implement and 2 months install. Total 12 months.
